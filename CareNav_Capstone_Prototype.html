Executive summary

A phased AI adoption plan for ED triage

CareNav Agent is a voice-enabled, agentic AI clinical decision-support system that assigns CTAS triage scores and recommends care pathways, with a mandatory human-in-the-loop confirmation on every recommendation at launch. This plan carries the case from pilot to full-network deployment across eight dimensions — context, technology, cost, security, change management, scaling, governance, and KPIs — each earning autonomy incrementally, under named accountability, never on a fixed calendar.

02 · Technology choice

Agentic AI on Azure OpenAI, Canada Central

Selected over a static rules-based CDSS and off-the-shelf third-party triage AI because clinical presentations are highly variable, and only a Canada-resident, contractually PHIPA-aligned agentic build meets the regulatory bar without a multi-year custom project.

Status quo — rule-based CDSS

Under-performs on 32% of ED presentations falling outside coded decision trees.

Off-the-shelf third-party AI

Faster to deploy, but no PHIPA-aligned Canadian residency guarantee and no HITL-mandatory architecture.

CareNav on Azure OpenAI selected

Canada Central residency, Healthcare DPA in place, compatible with Connected Care mandate.

07 · Governance and ethics

Named accountability, no single owner

PHIPA is the binding law; PIPEDA governs the Azure vendor relationship; Canada's Algorithmic Impact Assessment sets the governance benchmark. Health Canada's SaMD guidance becomes decisive only at the v3 autonomy threshold.

PHIPA — primaryPIPEDA — vendor layerCanada AIA — Level 3Health Canada SaMD — v3 onlyISO 42001 / EU AI Act — horizon

Fairness & nondiscrimination

Weekly equity analysis by demographic group; 5% disparity threshold triggers CASC review, given documented ED triage-downgrading disparities in Ontario data.

Transparency & explainability

Every recommendation includes a structured explainability summary — top symptom factors, confidence, pathway rationale.

Accountability

A named clinician, not the model, is accountable for every triage decision; CMO retains authority to halt deployment.

Privacy & data stewardship

Voice transcripts stripped of identifiers, never persisted beyond session; retraining uses de-identified synthetic data only.

09 · Conclusion

Autonomy earned, never assumed

Taken together, this plan operationalizes one principle across every section: agentic AI earns autonomy incrementally, under named human accountability, with oversight as the constant rather than the exception. CareNav's phased path — crawl, walk, run — gives Ontario Health a route to measurably shorter ED wait times that is fundable, auditable, and safe for the patients it is built to serve.
